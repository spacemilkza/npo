About LADGAC Rehabilitation Centre

At LADGAC Rehabilitation Centre, we are committed to helping individuals break free from the destructive cycles of drug addiction, gangsterism, and criminal behavior.



We Have 10 Years Experience

Our holistic rehabilitation program is designed to not only treat the symptoms but also address the root causes that lead to these destructive lifestyles.

Through personalized care, professional guidance, and a supportive environment, we help you rediscover your true potential and rebuild your life.

What We Offer

  • Detoxification & Substance Abuse Treatment
    We help individuals safely and effectively detox from harmful substances and provide ongoing support to maintain sobriety.
  • Psychological and Behavioral Therapy
    Our in-depth counseling sessions help individuals confront and resolve the emotional and psychological challenges that contribute to addiction and criminal behavior. Therapy options include cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), group therapy, and family counseling.
  • Gang Intervention and Reintegration
    Leaving gang life behind is never easy, but we offer tools, mentorship, and vocational training to help individuals find purpose and peace outside of gang affiliations. Our reintegration programs assist in building positive social networks and skills for reentry into society.
  • Crime Prevention and Rehabilitation
    We work closely with law enforcement, legal professionals, and community organizations to provide a second chance for those willing to change. Our crime rehabilitation programs focus on life skills training, anger management, conflict resolution, and employment assistance.
  • Life Skills and Vocational Training
    We equip our clients with practical skills needed to thrive in society. From education and job training to financial management and conflict resolution, we ensure that you leave with the tools for success.
